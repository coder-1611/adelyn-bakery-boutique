The boutique

A hand-painted corner of Paris

Step past the pastry case and you're on a painted Parisian street — flower carts, café awnings, the Eiffel Tower in the distance. Chef Annie opened Adelyn in 2019 after more than a decade in pastry, and everything in the case is still made from scratch, in-house, every single day.

— Chef Annie & the Adelyn team
