How it works

A conversation, not a form

Tell us the occasion

Call or come by with your date, guest count and any inspiration — a photo, a color, a theme, a feeling. We'll talk flavors while we're at it.

We design & bake

Chef Annie designs your cake around the celebration, then bakes and decorates it by hand — layers, fillings and finish all made from scratch.

Pick up & celebrate

Your cake is boxed and waiting at the boutique on the day. Add a matching box of macarons or cake pops for the dessert table.

Lead time

Custom work is baked to order — the earlier you call, the more we can do. For big dates like weddings, reach out a few weeks ahead.

Matching sweets

Round out the table with macaron towers, cupcakes and cake pops decorated to match your cake's palette and theme.

Every budget

From a single smash cake to a tiered showpiece — tell us what you're working with and we'll design to it. Quotes are always free.
